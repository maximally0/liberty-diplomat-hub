## DETAILED UI REQUIREMENTS

### A. Microsite / Register CTA

* **Register** button visible top-right on microsite page and above fold on Overview tab.
* Clicking opens sign-in modal if unauthenticated, otherwise goes straight to registration flow.
* Button states: Enabled / Disabled (if registration closed) / Loading.

### B. One-Time Quick Profile Capture (modal)

**Purpose:** collect user‚Äôs baseline info **once** on first registration (all fields required).
**Fields (single-page form):**

* Full name (text)
* Email (validated)
* Country (dropdown, searchable)
* City (text)
* Institution/School (autocomplete from mock institutions + ‚ÄúIndependent‚Äù option)
* Grade/Year (text)
* Phone (optional)
* Short bio (1‚Äì2 lines)
* Experience level (radio: Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
* Accept code of conduct checkbox (required)

**UX behavior:**

* Validate email format; show helpful errors.
* ‚ÄúSave & Continue‚Äù stores to local user state and closes modal.
* If profile already exists, show compact ‚ÄúConfirm your details‚Äù preview step instead of the full form.

### C. Registration Multi-step Flow (Event-level)

**Global UI:** Stepper at top (Profile ‚Üí Committee ‚Üí Portfolio ‚Üí Payment ‚Üí Review)

#### Step 1 ‚Äî Confirm Profile

* Readonly view of profile fields with ‚ÄúEdit‚Äù (opens profile modal).
* Show event-specific notes: registration open/close dates, fees, deadlines.
* CTA: Next

#### Step 2 ‚Äî Committee Selection & Country Preference

* Show committee list (cards) with: Committee name, short agenda, assigned chairs (small avatars), capacity (seats total / filled), difficulty level (Beginner/Advanced), and "Select" CTA.
* On selecting committee, show **country preference modal**: allow ranking 3 preferences with searchable country dropdowns. Add helper text: ‚ÄúWe‚Äôll try to allocate your top choice ‚Äî rank 1‚Äì3.‚Äù
* Display live capacity: if seats remaining == 0 ‚Üí show **Join Waitlist** CTA instead of Select.
* If online event: allow selecting virtual-only or in-person (if hybrid).
* Validate selection: require committee selection to proceed.

**Edge behavior:**

* If committee gets filled in the background (simulate with a mock 2s race), show conflict dialog and offer waitlist or choose another committee.

#### Step 3 ‚Äî Committee Portfolio (per-event)

* Minimal portfolio required inline: one short text area (300‚Äì500 chars) titled **‚ÄúCommittee Pitch / Position Summary‚Äù**. Microcopy: ‚ÄúSummarize your approach to this committee in 3‚Äì4 sentences.‚Äù
* Optional file upload: Position Paper (PDF up to 10MB) and supporting documents. Show drag-drop, progress bar, and preview.
* Allow saving as Draft and Continue Later.
* Show whether organizer requires position paper ‚Äî if required, mark field mandatory.

#### Step 4 ‚Äî Payment Options

* Show price breakdown: Registration fee, discounts (promo code), taxes, total.
* Payment options:

  1. **Pay Now** ‚Äî Simulate card payment modal: allow success and failure states. On success mark **Confirmed (Paid)**.
  2. **Pay Later (3-day hold)** ‚Äî Reserve seat for 72 hours. Show timer countdown (days/hours/mins). Generate a unique mock payment link and simulate email preview (contains link and expiry). Registration status: **Pending (Payment Due)**.
  3. **Invoice / Bank Transfer** ‚Äî open a form to request invoice (Name on invoice, Billing address). Mark status **Pending (Invoice)**. Organizer must mark paid in organizer view (UI-only).
* ‚ÄúPay Later‚Äù action: store reservation in local registrations.json with expiry timestamp. Also add a reminder notification scheduled (simulate) at 48 hours and 24 hours pre-expiry.
* ‚ÄúPay Now‚Äù actions show simulated spinner, outcome, and toast notifications.

**Edge cases:** If payment fails, allow retry and show actionable error messages. If Pay Later expires, seat auto-releases (simulate) and user notified.

#### Step 5 ‚Äî Review & Submit

* Show summary card of all choices (Profile, Committee, Country, Portfolio, Payment option).
* Edit buttons for each section.
* Final confirm button: Submit Registration.
* On submit show animated success screen with status and next steps (download invoice, contact delegate affairs, add to calendar).

### D. Post-Registration Dashboard & Actions (Delegate)

**Status states (UI):**

* **Confirmed (Paid)** ‚Äî shows Certificate ETA, calendar links, documents, ability to edit non-critical info until lock date.
* **Pending (Payment Due)** ‚Äî shows countdown to expiry, Pay Now button, link to payment email, ability to request a 2-day extension (contacts Delegate Affairs).
* **Waitlisted** ‚Äî shows position on waitlist (#X), option to remain on waitlist or withdraw.
* **Rejected** ‚Äî shows reason and appeal contact.
* **Withdrawn** ‚Äî shows re-registration link.

**Delegate actions:**

* **Edit Portfolio** (allowed up to organizer-specified deadline)
* **Pay Now** (if pending)
* **Contact Delegate Affairs** ‚Äî opens a prefilled message modal to organizer/delegate-affairs email with subject and body templates.
* **Invite Teammates** (if event allows team entries) ‚Äî send mock invites and show acceptance state.
* **Download Registration Receipt/PDF** ‚Äî simulated download.

**Notifications & Email previews:**

* Registration confirmation email (on Confirmed or Pending).
* Pay-later email with link and expiry.
* Reminder emails at 48h and 8h before expiry.
* Waitlist promotion email (if seat opens).
* All emails rendered as preview modals in the UI.

  * **Manually mark Paid** for bank transfer/invoice.
  * **Promote** waitlisted delegates if seat freed.
  * **Send Payment Reminder** (open small composer with mock send).

**Conflict resolution UI:** if capacity reduces, show conflict list and quick actions (Reassign / Waitlist / Refund). (All simulated)

### F. Edge Cases & Business Rules (must be simulated)

* **Concurrency race:** simulate scenario where two users attempt last seat ‚Äî implement conflict modal offering options.
* **Pay Later expiry:** when 72h passes (simulate with accelerated clock in prototype), release seat and update status of affected users.
* **Editing portfolio after submission:** allowed until organizer lock date; editing triggers "Updated portfolio" notification to chairs.
* **Duplicate accounts:** if same email tries to register twice, show merge prompt showing both profiles and allow merging (visual only).
* **Promo codes:** simulate 1-2 promo codes (e.g., PLFREE50 ‚Üí 50% off; STUDENT10 for students). Invalid codes show friendly error.
* **Late registration:** if user attempts to register after registration close time, show ‚ÄúLate Registration‚Äù modal: allow organizer override request via contact form.
